❓ What is this page?
  • This gauge estimates whether the overall US market is currently driven by fear (panic, selling) or greed (euphoria, chasing) — the same idea as CNN's famous Fear & Greed Index, computed transparently from live market data you can verify below.
  • Why it matters: the market's mood is the weather your stocks sail in. The same BUY signal on a stock is riskier during extreme greed (crowds are euphoric, corrections start there) and often more rewarding during extreme fear (the best long-term entries in history were made when this gauge was deep red).
  • The contrarian rule of thumb (Warren Buffett): "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful." Extreme readings are more informative than mid-range ones.
  • It is not a timing machine — markets can stay greedy or fearful for months. Use it as context, never as the whole decision.

❓ What is this page?
  • This page looks at your holdings as one portfolio instead of separate stocks. Enter shares + cost in any stock's Details panel to populate it.
  • Everything is converted to your chosen base currency using live exchange rates, so Thai baht and US dollar holdings finally add up to one number.
  • Allocation shows concentration risk — if one stock is a third of your money, your fate is tied to it. Correlation shows hidden duplication (two funds that move identically are really one bet). Dividend calendar estimates when your next payments arrive.
  • The history chart shows how your current holdings would have performed over the past year vs the S&P 500 — an honest benchmark check.
